What is Software Engineering?

Software engineering is delivering quality software that is on-time, on budget, defect-free and meets the requests of the client.

About the Software Engineering Department

  • RIT has the oldest Software Engineering BS degree program in the United States.
  • We are an ABET*-accredited program.
  • There are about 350 students in the department.
  • Previous students have gone to Microsoft, IBM, EA Games, Goodrich, Boeing, and many other companies.

* Accreditation Board of Engineering and Technology

Student Team Rooms

Equipped with computer-ready ceiling-mounted projector, a computer, whiteboard space, a meeting table, and comfortable seating for six persons, our 11 team-rooms are an integral part of our philosophy to emphasize the team.

General Use Lab

This lab is opened daily including weekends and is available to all software engineering majors and those from other programs who may be registered in one of our courses. The lab is outfitted with 40 workstations.

Society of Software Engineers Lab

This lab is designed to allow our newer students to network with other software engineering students who may provide answers to numerous questions.

Senior Projects Lab

Designed as a flexible team-project area, this lab is accessible 24 hours a day, 7 days a week by all of our seniors.

Real-Time and Embedded Systems Lab

Computing today is not restricted to devices that look like desktop computers. Building real-time and embedded systems in this lab will help you learn about one of the fastest-growing fields in computing.

Studio Labs

Designed to increase student-instructor interaction, these labs will enable you to understand the most complex concepts through hands-on exercises interleaved with lectures delivered in innovative formats.
